Welcome! The part one button will link to a page that will display xy, 12, and 89 in the fonts defined by the assignment. The Part two button will link you to a page which displays a form accepting 3 numeric inputs. Once three inputs are placed and validated, the text area of this page will display these numbers, mean, sum, and product as well as their min and max values. Finally there is the extra credit link which will take the you to a page containing a table with exchange rate data. This table will accept an input of US dollars and display its value converted by the exhange rate organized into a table.
